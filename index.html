About Me

Since September 2021, I have been pursuing a PhD at LTCI, Télécom Paris, under the supervision of Florence d’Alché-Buc and Matthieu Labeau. My research focuses on structured prediction, with applications in graph prediction and natural language processing.

I previously earned the French title of graduate engineer (Titre d’ingénieur diplômé) from Ecole d’ingénieurs Paris SJTU and a Master’s degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

Research Interests

I have a broad interest in Machine Learning and am currently focusing on:

  • Structure Prediction
  • Optimal Transport
  • Kernel Methods
  • Natural Language Processing
